# 📌 Project Overview

Developed a business intelligence and exploratory data analysis (EDA) project using Python to analyze Superstore sales data. The project focuses on identifying sales trends, profitability, customer behavior, regional performance, and discount strategies through interactive visualizations and business insights.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze sales and profit across different regions.
- Identify high-performing and low-performing product categories.
- Evaluate customer segments and purchasing behavior.
- Study shipping modes and their business impact.
- Discover seasonal sales trends.
- Support business decision-making using data.
